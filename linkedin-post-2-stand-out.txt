Searches for "how to make my resume stand out" are up 5,200% year over year.

People are desperate for answers. Here are 5 that actually work:

1. Mirror the JD keywords exactly — don't paraphrase, use their language
2. Put your Skills section above Experience — recruiters scan here first
3. Quantify everything — numbers beat adjectives every time
4. Lead with achievements, not responsibilities — what happened because of you?
5. Format for the machine first, human second — simple layout, standard headers
