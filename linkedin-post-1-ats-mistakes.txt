Built a free tool that tells you exactly what ATS systems see when they parse your resume.

Here are the 5 mistakes I saw most often — and how to fix them:

1. Wrong file format — DOCX beats PDF for most ATS parsers
2. Missing JD keywords — use the exact phrases from the job description
3. No quantified results — "increased sales" vs "grew revenue 40% in 6 months"
4. Inconsistent formatting — different date formats, bullet styles, section headers
5. One resume for every application — each role needs tailored keywords

Most of these take 10 minutes to fix and make a real difference in whether a recruiter scrolls past or clicks into your profile.
