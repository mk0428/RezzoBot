I tested 4 free ATS resume checkers so you don't have to.

Rezi, JobScan, Teal, and my own tool RezzoBot — same resume, same job description, head-to-head.

Here's the short version:

RezzoBot — best free option. 1 free scan/day, no credit card, detailed keyword-level feedback.
JobScan — most accurate parsing. 5 free scans/month. Good but limited free tier.
Teal — nicest UX. Organizes your whole job search. ATS score is solid but not as granular.
Rezi — good templates, weak free tier. More of a formatting tool than an analysis tool.

If you're applying to 1-2 jobs per week, RezzoBot's free tier covers you. If you're applying to 10+, JobScan's paid plan is worth it.
